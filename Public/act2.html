Rico's Pork Sinigang Recipe

Ingridients:

  • 500g pork(baboy),cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 packet of tamarind mix(sampalok mix) or 2-3 fresh tamarind fruits
  • 1 medium onion,sliced
  • 2 medium tomato,quartered
  • 1-inch ginger,sliced
  • 2-3 green chili peppers(siling mahaba)
  • 1 bunch kangkong(water spinach)or other leafy greens
  • 1 radish,sliced
  • 2-3 eggplants(talong),sliced
  • Salt and fish sauce(patris) to taste
  • 4 cups of water
  • Optional:okra(ladies' finger),balatong(string beans),or other veggies

Instructions:

  1. Boil pork in water with ginger,and onion until tender
  2. Add tamarind mix or fresh tamarind and simmer for 5-minutes
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until soft
  4. Add raddish,eggplant,and chili peppers
  5. Season with fish sauce and salt to taste
  6. Add kangkong and cook untik wilted
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice
